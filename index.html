Campus Life

A.E.C.S Kaiga

Was established in 1988

The school is located at NPCIL Kaiga colony. AECS Kaiga was established for children of employees of NPCIL Kaiga. It runs classes from pre-prep to 12th. The school has a physics lab, a chemistry lab, a biology and a maths lab. The sub-languages taught are Kannada and Hindi/Sanskrit. The school has two wings one for the primary and the other for the secondary.
